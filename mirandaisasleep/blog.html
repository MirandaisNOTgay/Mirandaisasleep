My Very Second Blog Post! - 2025-02-10

I think I want to talk about Gorillaz today! Music is a big part of who I am, and I really want to share it with people.
My first real deep dive into Gorillaz was when I got a bass. Nobody is a wonderful musician on the first day, and so I did as most do and searched up easy songs to play. The Feel Good Inc. bassline really inspired me to take a look at other Gorillaz songs. Surprisingly, most have this catchy yet simplistic bassline that is missing from music these days. For example, if any bassists want reccomendations, "on melancholy hill", "o green world", and "the pink phantom" all have easy yet fun bass riffs. My favorite Gorillaz video features a live of, "o green world," and in that one, you can really feel the bass. Not to mention The bassist is in front, absolutely shredding. Watch her go! She really encapsulates that rough Gorillaz theme Demon Days goes for.
